§02 · 10+ Years in Executive Search

Meet the Founder.

// Founder & Recruitment Lead

CA Ketul Patel

CA Ketul Patel is a Chartered Accountant turned executive search specialist with over a decade placing marketing, sales, BD, and operations leaders at jewelry and consumer brands.

His financial background gives him a different lens: he reads compensation structures that CTC slips hide, and he understands what a senior hire actually costs versus what a brand budgets.

Having worked across jewelry, D2C, and organized retail, Ketul built Boardroom CXO around one conviction: the best candidates are not applying anywhere. They are running the functions you need to build, inside competitor companies. Reaching them requires research, timing, and a confidential approach.

Every search at Boardroom CXO starts with market mapping and ends with a mandate defined clearly enough that the hire stays.
